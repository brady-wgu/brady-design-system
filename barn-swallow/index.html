LIME and SWALLOW are in the same warm-light zone but fight each other — LIME has a cool-green cast that jars against the warm yellow/orange/ember barn swallow progression. SWALLOW slots naturally into the gradient; LIME doesn't.

Recommendation: drop LIME

It has no semantic role, it clashes visually with the barn swallow warm progression, and it competes with SWALLOW on covers. Removing it leaves the WGU palette as pure blues (MIDNIGHT → NAVY → BS_MID → BLUE → SKY → ICE) — which is exactly what you said is the critical branding signal. If the self-test needs updating after removal, that's a 10-second change.
