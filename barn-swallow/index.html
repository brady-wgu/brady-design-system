Callout System

All Eight Variants

PDev colors replaced throughout. NOTE and KEY INSIGHT retain WGU blues. All others are now barn swallow.

KEY INSIGHT

The strongest signal in the system. NAVY bar and tint — unchanged from WGU brand. Use for primary findings.

NAVY #002855 bar · TINT_NAVY bg · NAVY label ≈ 8.2:1 AAA

NOTE

Context worth noticing. BLUE bar and label — unchanged WGU brand. Use for supplemental context.

BLUE #0070F0 bar · TINT_BLUE bg · BLUE label ≈ 3.9:1 (large text)

BOTTOM LINE

The actionable TL;DR. BLUE bar on PAPER background — neutral and clean, stands apart from NOTE.

BLUE #0070F0 bar · PAPER #F8F9FA bg · BLUE label ≈ 4.9:1 AA

DEFINITION

Term introduction. BS_MID — deep steel blue — replaces the PDev purple. Same informational energy, native to the barn swallow palette.

BS_MID #1E2E56 bar · TINT_MID bg · BS_MID label ≈ 10:1 AAA

WARNING

Risks and caveats. SWALLOW (Gape Yellow) replaces PDev gold as the bar color. Label uses BS_EMBER since SWALLOW fails as text on any light background.

SWALLOW #FFCC00 bar · TINT_SWALLOW bg · BS_EMBER label ≈ 3.9:1 (large text)

CAUTION

Do-not-proceed. BS_EMBER replaces PDev red. Warm ember-red reads as stop/danger without the clinical harshness of pure red.

BS_EMBER #C2592A bar · TINT_EMBER bg · BS_EMBER label ≈ 3.7:1 (large text)

QUOTE

Attributed excerpts. BS_TAWNY (cinnamon) replaces PDev teal. Warm and earthy — fits the attribution context. Label uses INK since BS_TAWNY fails on its own tint.

BS_TAWNY #D97443 bar · TINT_TAWNY bg · INK label ≈ 19:1 AAA

FINDINGS · SKY on MIDNIGHT ≈ 8.9:1 AAA — not SWALLOW, which fails on dark bands

SWALLOW fails contrast on NAVY/MIDNIGHT as text — that's why section divider numerals stay SKY (#46B1EF). SWALLOW is safe in the four non-semantic accent slots: stat numbers on light bg, key-finding numerals, figure highlight, cover art third color.
